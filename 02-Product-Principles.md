# 01 - Vision

## Product Vision

Kendo Companion exists to become the most trusted digital companion for a kendoka's long-term development.

The product helps committed practitioners capture learning, organise knowledge, and prepare deliberately for their next keiko.

It does not replace Sensei, senpai, dojo instruction, seminars, books, or traditional learning. It strengthens them by preserving lessons that would otherwise be forgotten.

## Mission

Help serious kendoka remember, connect, and apply what they learn.

## Core Problem

Committed kendoka receive many pieces of coaching and develop many insights over years of practice. Much of this learning is forgotten, disconnected, or never intentionally reviewed before the next keiko.

The application solves this by turning post-keiko reflection into deliberate preparation.

## Product Loop

```text
Train
↓
Reflect
↓
Knowledge grows
↓
Current Intention updates
↓
Prepare
↓
Train again
```

## Success Statement

The product succeeds when users naturally think:

> Before every keiko, I open the app for thirty seconds to remind myself what I am working on.
