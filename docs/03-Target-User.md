# 03 - Target User

## Primary User

The primary user is the committed kendoka.

Typical profile:

- Around 1 Kyu to 3 Dan
- Trains regularly
- Wants continual improvement
- Receives coaching from Sensei and senpai
- Thinks about kendo outside the dojo
- May record training video
- May attend seminars and gradings
- Wants deliberate practice rather than simply accumulating hours

## Secondary Users

Beginners may use the product, but the product is not primarily designed as a beginner teaching app.

Senior practitioners may use the product, but the product is not primarily designed around 6th or 7th Dan workflows.

## User Need

The committed kendoka needs a reliable place to preserve coaching, reflect on training, organise technical development, and arrive at the next keiko with one clear intention.
