# 10 - Moments Specification

## Purpose

Moments provide visual learning evidence.

They are not a video library.

## Rule

One Moment = One Lesson.

## Duration

5-10 seconds.

## Supported Types

Version 1 should support:

- GIF
- Short video clip
- Photo
- YouTube timestamp

## Moment Purposes

A Moment should be categorised by purpose:

- Success
- Correction
- Demonstration
- Breakthrough

## Storage Strategy

Avoid hosting long training videos in Version 1.

Prefer:

- Local files
- User-provided clips/GIFs
- YouTube timestamps

Long-term cloud storage is optional and future-facing.
